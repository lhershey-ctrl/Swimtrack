Dr. Klaus Rudolph's German age-graded points table (8 Jahre through 18+/offen, both sexes). Turns any swim time into a 1-20 score for that specific age, so progress stays comparable across age-group jumps. Upload the official PDF whenever a new "Basis" year is published — it's parsed entirely in the browser.

📄 USA Motivational Standards

        
Sign in (Analyze tab) to see the currently published standards.

      

      ▾ upload new data
    
    

  
USA Swimming's junior age-group tiers (B / BB / A / AA / AAA / AAAA), ages 10-18, both sexes. Only the metric tables (SCM/LCM) are used — the source PDF's yards (SCY) tables are skipped since this app tracks meters only. Upload whenever new standards are published.
Source: usaswimming.org/times/time-standards.
